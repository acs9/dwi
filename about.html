The Story...

India's First IT Ezine - DataWorld India (DWI) founded in late 1998, started out as an e-magazine, and provided content for various IT magazines and newspapers. The focus was primarily on technical reviews, new product launches, and emerging technologies. DWI also worked with the press covering IT exhibitions. We have worked with HT Tech, CNBC, and also appeared on CNBC and TMG (TV) on technology topics. We have been featured in various print and web publications as well as IBM's 'Other voices'.

Digital Transformation Specialists - Moving past the y2k dotcom period, DWI moved focus to web presence, helping individuals and small businesses get their businesses online. Over the years, DWI has supported, and grown with our clients, enabling them to transform business operatations and help serve their users.

Focus on Micro and SME - Our clientele includes individuals and organisations from USA, Europe and APAC region, but the primary focus remains the Indian peninsula.

Today, you will find a 'web developer' practically everywhere. The question is, who do you trust with your information, your business, your future plans? Who will be around when you need something, anything done, that relates to your online business? Can you be sure they will not leave you in suspense, when you really need your website online?
DWI has been around since 1996 and maintains the same level of high integrity since, and continues to work from proven, time-tested, first principles. If you are looking to get your business online, backed by nearly 20 years experience, please don't hesitate to contact us.
